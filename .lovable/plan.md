

## Lovable Homepage Replica with Particle Shader Background

### Overview
A pixel-perfect replica of the Lovable homepage featuring a particle shader background instead of the color gradient, with full dark mode support.

---

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Fixed top navbar with logo ("Lovable" with icon), nav links (Solutions, Resources, Enterprise, Pricing, Community)
- Solutions and Resources with dropdown chevrons
- Right side: "Log in" and "Get started" buttons with border styling
- Subtle hover effects on all links and buttons

### 2. Hero Section with Particle Background
- Full-screen hero section with the particle shader component as background
- **Light mode**: Dark/black particles on the existing gradient-style background
- **Dark mode**: White particles on a black background, particles move slightly faster
- Centered content: "New" badge with "Introducing a smarter Lovable →" announcement pill
- Large heading: "Build something Lovable"
- Subtitle: "Create apps and websites by chatting with AI"

### 3. Chat Input Box
- Dark semi-transparent chat input area with rounded corners and subtle shadow
- Placeholder text: "Ask Lovable to create a prototy..."
- Bottom toolbar with: "+" attachment button (left), "Plan" pill button (blue), voice icon, and send button (right)
- Matches the exact styling from the screenshot

### 4. Discover Templates Section
- Section heading "Discover templates" with subtitle "Start your next project with a template"
- "View all →" link on the right
- 4-column grid of template cards (2 rows, 8 templates total)
- Each card: rounded preview image, title, and subtitle description
- Templates include: Ecommerce Store, Architect Portfolio, Personal Blog, Fashion Blog, Visual Landing Page, Lifestyle Blog, Event Platform, Personal Portfolio

### 5. Discover Apps Section
- Section heading "Discover apps" with subtitle "Explore what others are building"
- "View all →" link on the right
